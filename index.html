About Ellisa

Ellisa is a musician from the Metro Detroit area that recently relocated to Indianaplis, Indiana. She has been singing as long as she can remember. "My mom likes to say that I came home fro the hospital humming".

She started doing community theatre at a young age and then began taking private vocal lessons in middle school. Her training included classical, pop, and musical theatre. She competed in The American Guild of Music competitions for four years. Her thoughts on her training were that she had to learn the rules to break them. She had to learn to control her voice so she could perform the best.

After graduating from high school she took a break from singing but the call to perform was too strong so she began looking for venues. After a chance performance with a band from Nashville at Tin Roof Detroit they began booking her on a regular basis. Since then Ellisa has performed at different locations throughout the Metro Detroit area.

Her favorite types of music to perform are Country or Rock but her voice lies in R&B. In her own words "I try to perform whatever the crowd wants to hear." But whether it's Country, Rock, Pop, or R&B everyone is sure to have a good time. With her own brand of sarcasm and talent Ellisa gives a show you won't forget soon.
